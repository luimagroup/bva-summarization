The veteran served in Vietnam at an air base in which stressful events, including enemy attacks with resulting loss of life, occurred.  2.
The veteran testified at his September 1993 personal hearing concerning the service events in Vietnam, described above, and post service events including a record of current treatment for PTSD every two weeks.
In the case at hand, the veteran has described an event where the enemy attacked an air base where the veteran was stationed at night in early December 1966 with resulting loss of considerable life of the enemy as well as the wounding of American soldiers and the death of at least three soldiers, and damage to the air field.
