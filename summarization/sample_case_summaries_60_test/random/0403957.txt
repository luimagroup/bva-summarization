On appeal from the Department of Veterans Affairs Regional Office in Hartford, Connecticut.
The issue is Entitlement to service connection for post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD).
The veteran had verified active duty from March 1963 to March 1965.
To date, the service department has not been able to verify the veteran's alleged active duty in Vietnam.
In the Board's opinion further development is in order to determine if the veteran did in fact serve in Vietnam.
The efforts to obtain such records should be documented.
By this remand, the Board intimates no opinion as to any final outcome warranted.
