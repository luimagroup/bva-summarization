On appeal from the Department of Veterans Affairs Regional Office in Muskogee, Oklahoma.
The issue is Entitlement to service connection for an acquired psychiatric disorder to include post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD).
The veteran had active duty service from November 1962 to November 1965.
The Board finds VA should contact the veteran to determine if there are any outstanding treatment records and, if so, should take appropriate steps to obtain the evidence identified by the veteran if possible.
After securing the necessary release, VA should obtain these records.
Thereafter, and after completing any further development determined to be warranted by the RO or the AMC, the claim of entitlement to service connection for an acquired psychiatric disorder to include PTSD should be adjudicated based on all the evidence in the claims file.
